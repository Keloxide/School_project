ABOUT

Bright Child School is an educational instituition founded on core values and a strong curriculum. At Bright Child, pupils are carefully guided in a solid core of academic subjects from primary 1 - 6. they include Mathematics, English Language, Science, Music, Physical and Health Education and Computer. An education at Bright Child emphasises deductive reasoning and critical analytic skills bearing in mind that education is not limited to the classroom but continues in all our life experiences. Brief History:

Our teachers ensures that pupils' individual academic needs are never overlooked and that all pupils have the opportunity to achieve their full potential. we believe that education is incomplete without life skills and values, we therefore go to great lengths. For more information about our school, we invite you to visit us or call for an appointment to tour our facilities. emphasise values like honesty, diligence and responsibility to mention a few. HISTORIC Bright Child School was founded on january 10 2000 by Mrs FA Achioya. At the very core of its foundation is the passionate belief that the right blend of academic knowledge and ethical beliefs can change the world for good. After the annulment of the June 12, 1993 elections in Nigeria, the Nigerian economy was in a near-comatose state as the country's political situation was in chaos. The only way to redeem the nation and protect its future was to teach young people the right values so that they would in turn, affect others until Nigerians start thinking and acting in the collective interest of all. so far, the vision is paying great dividends as there are Bright Child students who are University Graduates. In the years to come we hope to have bright Child alumni in positions of influence in Nigeria who can make and implement policies that improve people's lives and make Nigeria a standard in good governance. At Bright Child, we are Determined to change Nigeria by influencing Families through their Children.
